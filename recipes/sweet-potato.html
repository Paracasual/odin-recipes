Baked Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes, scrubbed clean
  • Olive oil, optional
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425ยบ. On a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil, prick sweet potatoes all over with a fork. Rub the outsides with a small amount of olive oil if you intend to eat the skins.
  2. Bake until tender, 45 to 50 minutes.
  3. Let cool, then split the tops open with a knife and top with a pat of butter.
